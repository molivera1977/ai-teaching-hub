📋 How the Test Works

👤 Step 1 — Sign In

Choose your name from the list, then enter your student number and tap Sign In.

📋 Step 2 — Complete All Four Parts

The test has four parts that must be completed in order: Vocabulary, Comprehension, Written Response, and Cloze. Each part unlocks only after you finish and submit the one before it. You have one attempt per part. After you finish the last part, you will see a summary of all your scores.

⚠️ No Answer Feedback

You will not see whether your answers are right or wrong during the test. Read each question carefully, choose your best answer, and confirm it. Your score is revealed at the end of each part.

📖 Reading Timer

When each question appears, the answer choices are locked for 12 seconds. Use that time to read the question and all the choices carefully before making your selection.

⏱️ 12 second read lock

⏱ Overall Timer

A timer runs during each part. It is not a countdown — there is no time limit. It only tracks how long you take so your teacher can see your progress.
